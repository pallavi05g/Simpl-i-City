Lingo

Since Bangalore is a Metropolitan city, its culture base is diverse. The people here come from all parts of the country and you can find a lot of expats as well. “Silicon Valley of India” or “Garden City” as it is fondly called, this place is the home to a wide array of Indian languages. Most of the folks out here are comfortable with English. However, there is no harm in learning a few key phrases in the native language, Kannada.

Local language: Kannada

National language: Hindi

You better start looking into these local language phrases if you plan to stay here for a longer time. Though Hindi will do, knowing the local language will help.
