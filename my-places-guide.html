Places

Wondering what are the places of interest in the city?
These are some places you must see when you come to this city.

Don't forget your camera!!!

Vidhana Soudha

Vidhana Soudha
Dr Ambedkar Road, Sampangi Ramnagar, Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights: 

– The Vidhana Soudha, located in Bengaluru, is the seat of the state legislature of Karnataka;
– Dravidian architecture

Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lal Bagh Botanical Garden
Lal Bagh Road, Lalbagh, Mavalli,
Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights:

- Lal Bagh or Lal Bagh Botanical Gardens, meaning The Red Garden in English, is a well known botanical garden in southern Bangalore, India.

HAL Musuem

HAL Musuem
Varthur Rd,
Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights:

- HAL Aerospace Museum is India's first aerospace museum located at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited premises, in Bangalore.

Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace

Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace
Albert Victor Road, Chamrajpet,
Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights:

- Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace, in Bangalore, India, is an example of Indo-Islamic architecture and was the summer residence of the Mysorean ruler Tipu Sultan. 

Bannerghatta National Park

Bannerghatta National Park
Bannerghatta Road, Bannerghatta,
Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights:

- Bannerghatta National Park, near Bangalore, Karnataka, southern India, was founded in 1971 and declared as a national park in 1974. In 2002 a portion of the park, became a biological reserve, the Bannerghatta Biological Park.

Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace
Near Mount Carmel Institute of Management,
Palace Road, Vasanth Nagar,
Bangalore, Karnataka

Highlights:

–Bangalore Palace, a palace located in Bangalore, India, was built by Rev. Garrett, who was the first Principal of the Central High School in Bangalore, now known as Central College.
-It is mistakenly believed to be a replica of the Windsor Castle in England.
